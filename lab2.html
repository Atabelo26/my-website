17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

  1. SDG 1 – No Poverty

    Goal:
    End poverty in all its forms everywhere.
    Ensure that all people, everywhere, have equal access to basic services and economic opportunities.

  2. SDG 2 – Zero Hunger

    Goal:
    End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.
    Ensure everyone has access to sufficient and nutritious food to lead a healthy life.

  3. SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-being

    Goal:
    Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.
    Improve health outcomes worldwide and ensure everyone has access to health services and well-being opportunities.

  4. SDG 4 – Quality Education

    Goal:
    Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.
    Provide quality education at all levels, ensuring everyone has access to learning and skill-building opportunities.

  5. SDG 5 – Gender Equality

    Goal:
    Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.
    Eliminate all forms of discrimination and violence based on gender.

  6. SDG 6 – Clean Water and Sanitation

    Goal:
    Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.
    Provide access to clean water and sanitation for everyone, ensuring that water resources are sustainably managed.

  7. SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy

    Goal:
    Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all.
    Ensure universal access to affordable, sustainable energy sources, and promote energy efficiency.

  8. SDG 8 – Decent Work and Economic Growth

    Goal:
    Promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all.
    Foster sustainable economic growth, ensure equal opportunities for decent work, and reduce youth unemployment.

  9. SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure

    Goal:
    Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.
    Support the development of infrastructure that promotes innovation and economic progress, particularly in developing countries.

  10. SDG 10 – Reduced Inequalities

    Goal:
    Reduce inequality within and among countries.
    Promote equality of opportunity for all, and address inequalities between and within countries.

  11. SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities

    Goal:
    Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.
    Promote sustainable urban development and ensure that cities are resilient to environmental challenges.

  12. SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production

    Goal:
    Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.
    Encourage the use of resources efficiently, and reduce waste to promote a sustainable and circular economy.

  13. SDG 13 – Climate Action

    Goal:
    Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.
    Take immediate and effective steps to mitigate climate change and its impacts on the environment and society.

  14. SDG 14 – Life Below Water

    Goal:
    Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development.
    Protect and restore marine ecosystems to maintain the health of oceans and safeguard biodiversity.

  15. SDG 15 – Life on Land

    Goal:
    Protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, halt and reverse land degradation, and halt biodiversity loss.

  16. SDG 16 – Peace and Justice Strong Institutions

    Goal:
    Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions at all levels.

  17. SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals

    Goal:
    Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development.
